Daily Schedule

🏕   Every camper goes home with something they made   🏕
What you'll make & do

Camp Activities

Every day is packed with real builds, real launches, and real messes — the good kind.

Monday
🎒

Customize Your Backpack

Use the Cricut to cut iron-on vinyl patches and designs. Names, logos, camp artwork — press it on and wear it all week.

Monday
🚀

Pneumatic Paper Rocket SOS

Roll and launch a paper rocket carrying a distress message. Iterate on fins, nose cone, and tube to maximize range.

Tuesday
🪢

Paracord Bracelet or Keychain

Learn the cobra knot and make a paracord survival bracelet or keychain to keep. Overflow time available Wednesday morning.

Tuesday
⛺

Weatherproof Tent Build

Build a shelter from tarps, rope, and stakes that must survive a real garden hose rainstorm test. Earn coin if yours passes.

Wednesday
💡

Paper Lantern with LED

Fold a paper lantern and wire a coin battery + LED to light it from the inside. Every lantern glows or it's back to the bench.

Wednesday
🏃

Design a Camp Obstacle Course

Design and build a camping-themed obstacle course, then test it with fellow campers. Most creative earns bonus coin.

Thursday
🏷

Camp Stickers

Design and print a full sheet of custom camp stickers — Innovation Outpost logo, your team name, personal designs.

Thursday
🧭

Micro:bit Compass

Code a working compass using the Micro:bit magnetometer in MakeCode. Calibrate outside, run the bearing drill, use it Friday.

Thu – Fri
🔨

Survival Gadget Challenge

Design and build a camping survival gadget over two days. Showcase it Friday for judge feedback and camp coin awards.

Friday
🔐

Micro:bit Escape Room

Use your compass to navigate STEM puzzle stations. Solve circuits, codes, and physical challenges. Camp coin on every puzzle.

Friday
🍦

Ice Cream in a Baggie

Make it in the morning, freeze it during the escape room, eat it cold after the battle royale — chocolate chips included!

All Week — Early Finishers
🎵
Paper Whistle
Fold and tune a paper whistle — loudest, highest, or strangest pitch wins.
🥏
Flying Disc
Make a paper flying disc and test it for distance and flight stability.
🐸
Origami Animals
Follow instructions to fold a crane, frog, fox, or other origami animal.
🎯
Q-Tip Crossbow
Build a mini crossbow from craft sticks and rubber bands. Fire q-tips for accuracy.
🎳
Catapult Bowling
Build a mini catapult and knock down clothespin pins. Most pins down wins.
🦉
TP Tube Owl
Turn a toilet paper tube into an owl with folded ears, googly eyes, and paper wings.
All Week
🪙

Camp Coin & Store

Earn coin by completing builds and challenges. Camp Champ shoutouts and the store open every day before water play.
